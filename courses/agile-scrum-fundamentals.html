Introduction

The Agile Scrum Fundamentals training program provides an understanding of fundamental principles of agile and scrum methodologies. This program has been approved by Project Management Institute (PMI) USA and the participants will be awarded with a certificate endorsed by PMI USA. PMP Certificants can earn 10 PDUs (Professional Development Units) towards maintaining their certification by taking part in this program

Trainer Profile

Trainer: Dr. Duminda Weeraratne MBA (Sri J), PMP,PMI-RMP,PMI-ACP,PMI-PBA,CSM
A trainer, speaker, coach and management consultant with two decades of industry experience specialising in project management and one decade of experience in training and coaching professionals from IT, Finance, Healthcare, Real Estate, Pharmaceutical, Leisure, and Manufacturing industries. Popular for his unique simple and practical training delivery approach. Trained over 4500 executives of leading corporations on project management. Conducted over 80 public and in house workshops on project management and general management. Has produced over 700 PMPs, 200 CAPM credential holders and over 200 Agile Certificants during the last 12 years. Authored a popular book on Project Management. Was a visiting faculty member of MBA in Project Management (University of Southern Queensland Sri Lankan Branch) 2005 to 2006 and MBA program (University of Colombo) from 2008 to 2015. Research interests are in the fields of IT project management, IT industry development and Agile transition.

Objectives

After training, your project team members will be able to


  • 1
    Learn fundamental principles of Scrum and Agile methodologies
  • 2
    Gain skills required to practice Agile Scrum
  • 3
    Learn to use Scrum Artifacts

Target Participants

Project Managers and Team Leaders.

Our Learning Process

Participants in the program will be assisted by PMS’s facilitators on their learning journey with:


  • 1
    Short presentations on the selected agile scrum topics.
  • 2
    Participants in small teams of 4-5 members, carry out practical exercises applying agile scrum concepts
  • 3
    Participants discuss and find solutions to difficulties they may encounter during real life implementation
  • 4
    Templates and formats required for case analysis will be provided to all participants

Course Contents

  • 1
    Introduction to Agile
  • 2
    Agile Manifesto Values, Agile Principles, Benefits of Agile, Agile vs Waterfall
  • 3
    Introduction to Scrum
  • 4
    Evolution of Scrum, Foundations of Scrum, Scrum Process, Scrum Values
  • 5
    Scrum Roles
  • 6
    The Product Owner, the Scrum Master, Team
  • 7
    Scrum Artifacts(Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, Burn Down Chart)
  • 8
    Time Boxes(Sprint, Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, Sprint Retrospective)
