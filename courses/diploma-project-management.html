Approved by Project Management Institute(PMI) - USA and Conducted Conforming to PMI Quality Assurance Criteria


PMS's Diploma in Project Management will take you from concept to completion covering every step of effective project initiation, planning, executing, controlling and closing. It provides a broad and practical curriculum that builds confidence and skills necessary to tackle the challenges of directing projects in a broad range of work environments.

Online Training with a Classroom Feeling

All the diploma lessons are recorded in a classroom environment and discussions are supported with explanations on a whiteboard without power point presentations. Concepts are explained in a simple and a methodical manner relating to real life project scenarios.


Scaleable Project Management Templates for You to Use

Templates for all the key areas of the project which can be tailored to your project and organizational environment. This will make sure that you will not miss out any important element of your project plan.


Expert Trainer Support

Once a week the trainees can attend a live doubt-clearing session and get all their queries cleared.

Trainer Profile

Trainer: Dr. Duminda Weeraratne MBA (Sri J), PMP,PMI-RMP,PMI-ACP,PMI-PBA,CSM
A trainer, speaker, coach and management consultant with two decades of industry experience specialising in project management and one decade of experience in training and coaching professionals from IT, Finance, Healthcare, Real Estate, Pharmaceutical, Leisure, and Manufacturing industries. Popular for his unique simple and practical training delivery approach. Trained over 4500 executives of leading corporations on project management. Conducted over 80 public and in house workshops on project management and general management. Has produced over 700 PMPs, 200 CAPM credential holders and over 200 Agile Certificants during the last 12 years. Authored a popular book on Project Management. Was a visiting faculty member of MBA in Project Management (University of Southern Queensland Sri Lankan Branch) 2005 to 2006 and MBA program (University of Colombo) from 2008 to 2015. Research interests are in the fields of IT project management, IT industry development and Agile transition.

Objectives

At the end of the program the participants will be able to;


  • 1
    Assess the organizational context for projects and apply project management principles to workplace initiatives.
  • 2
    Understand the concept of project program, portfolio and PMO management and learn to align initiatives with strategic objectives.
  • 3
    Gain knowledge and skills required to carry out feasibility study.
  • 4
    Manage scope, time and cost to deliver promised results.
  • 5
    Develop techniques for monitoring, controlling and reporting project progress.
  • 6
    Manage stakeholders effectively including the project sponsor, client, team members and others.
  • 7
    Learn to prepare business case and project proposals.
  • 8
    Identify and analyze project risks and develop responses to address risks.
  • 9
    Master negotiations, and conflict resolution techniques.
  • 10
    Plan procurement and manage contracts in a cost efficient manner.
  • 11
    Apply quality management concepts to ensure that results meet the specifications.
  • 12
    Become an effective project leader who can enhance organizational performance.
  • 13
    Manage Projects effectively and deliver solutions using the processes of the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


I am not an Engineer . Can I do the Diploma in Project Management course ?

Project management is necessary for all professionals. Projects are undertaken by professionals in HR management, marketing management, finance management, supply chain management, operations management, etc. Most of the activities we undertake in our lives are projects. Therefore anyone can follow this course.


Is this course recognized worldwide ?

This is a Project Management Institute (PMI) ® , USA approved training program. It is conducted according to their quality criteria. As a Global Registered Education Provider (REP) with PMI®, USA we are authorized to conduct this program and the participants will receive a certificate endorsed by PMI® USA.


I am not living close to your institute. Can I also follow the course ?

This online program is meant for people like you. You can learn online and still get the classroom feeling.
